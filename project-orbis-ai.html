Your everyday life – organized safely and intelligently. Orbis is an AI-based agent system that supports people with specialized digital buddies in all areas of life. Instead of one universal assistant, multiple buddies work in parallel, bundle relevant information, take on tasks and coordinate with each other.

In times of increasing intransparency, Orbis focuses on control, traceability and trust – for a clearly structured, noticeably relieved everyday life.
